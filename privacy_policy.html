Privacy Policy for Earphoria

Last Updated: December 27, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Earphoria ("we", "our", or "the app") handles your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Last.fm account credentials (if you choose to enable Last.fm scrobbling)
  • Playlists and music preferences you create within the app
  • Downloaded music files stored locally on your device

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information (device model, operating system version)
  • App usage statistics and crash reports
  • Network connectivity status (to determine online/offline mode)

1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do NOT collect personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • We do NOT track your location
  • We do NOT collect payment information (the app is free)
  • We do NOT sell or share your data with third parties for advertising purposes

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide music streaming and playback functionality
  • Enable offline music playback through local storage
  • Sync listening history with Last.fm (only if enabled by you)
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs
  • Maintain and enhance app features

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

  • All downloaded music files are stored locally on your device
  • Playlists and preferences are stored in the local app database
  • You can delete this data by clearing app data or uninstalling the app

3.2 Third-Party Services

The app integrates with the following services:

  • YouTube – for music streaming (subject to YouTube’s Privacy Policy)
  • Last.fm – for scrobbling (only if enabled)
  • Billboard and other chart providers – for displaying music charts

We do not control these third-party services and recommend reviewing their respective privacy policies.

4. Permissions Required

  • INTERNET – stream music and fetch content
  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE / READ_MEDIA_AUDIO – access local music
  • WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE – download music for offline playback
  • WAKE_LOCK – keep device awake during playback
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE – background playback
  • ACCESS_NOTIFICATION_POLICY – playback controls in notifications

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell, trade, or rent your information.

We may share information only:

  • With Last.fm, if you enable scrobbling
  • When required by law
  • To protect our rights, safety, or property

6. Children’s Privacy

Earphoria does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has shared personal information, please contact us.

7. Data Retention

  • All data stays on your device until you delete it
  • You can clear app data anytime via device settings
  • Uninstalling the app removes all stored data

8. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access or delete local data anytime
  • Disable Last.fm scrobbling
  • Revoke permissions via device settings
  • Uninstall the app to remove everything

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be posted in-app and reflected in the “Last Updated” date.

10. Open Source Notice

Earphoria is an open-source project forked from BloomeeTunes by @iamhemantindia. The source code is publicly available for transparency.

Earphoria does not host music content. All music is streamed from third-party sources, primarily YouTube. Users are responsible for complying with copyright laws and third-party terms of service.
